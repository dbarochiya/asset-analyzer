## Features 

- capital injection/removal should show on the main graph of accounts (where we have the nav and total invested)

- account summary should be kept individual for each account and not on the main page

- where should I put my money - this tool should help me with that based on the data 

- **Rolling 12-Month XIRR** 
Instead of cumulative XIRR (gets more stable over time), show XIRR of the *last 12 months* as a moving window. This tells you "how am I doing recently" not "how did I do historically"

- **Inflation-Adjusted Returns** 
Show XIRR net of CPI (India ~6%). A 10% XIRR with 6% inflation = only 4% real return. 

- **Benchmark XIRR** 
Not just NAV comparison but "if you had invested the same cashflows in Nifty, what XIRR would you have gotten?" Uses same dates & amounts as your actual cashflows. 

- **Export to PDF** 
Single-page report card with all KPIs and charts. 

- **Benchmarking dynamically**
Provide options to add comparision dynamically
